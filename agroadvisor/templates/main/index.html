Frequently Asked Questions

AgroAdvisor is an all-in-one web platform designed to help farmers make smarter, data-driven decisions. It provides tools for crop recommendation, 90-day price forecasting, and a marketplace to sell your produce directly to buyers.

Our Crop Recommender uses advanced machine learning models. You enter your farm's district, seasonal weather, and soil data (like N, P, K levels). The system analyzes this against historical yield data to recommend the top 5 crops that are best suited for your specific conditions.

The Price Predictor uses historical market data, arrival volumes, and weather patterns to forecast prices 90 days in the future. While it's a powerful estimation tool to help you plan, actual market prices can vary based on real-time supply, demand, and other unforeseen events. The chart also shows you the model's confidence (R2 score) for transparency.

You can browse the homepage and the Marketplace as a guest. However, to use the personalized Crop Recommender, Price Predictor, or to sell your own products in the Marketplace, you must register for a free account.
